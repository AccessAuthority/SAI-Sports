Our Story

The company was established in 2003 by renowned national cricket player Mr. Alok Puri under the name of Sai Sports India. With a strong foundation rooted in sports, the company has grown to become a leading name in the sports manufacturing industry. Since its inception, the company has prided itself on delivering top-quality sports apparel and equipment to athletes and sports organizations across the nation.

Sai Sports India operates its own manufacturing facility, ensuring complete control over the production process. The company’s advanced infrastructure allows it to manufacture a wide variety of sports-related products, including specialized sports clothing and technical equipment. This in-house production capability enables the company to maintain stringent quality standards while ensuring timely delivery to its clients.

The company specializes in producing a wide range of clothing segments, including sports apparel, and provides a comprehensive array of technical equipment for the sports industry. The diverse product offerings cater to both the athletic and recreational sports markets, meeting the needs of amateur and professional athletes alike.

With a manufacturing capacity of 5,000 tracksuits and 10,000 T-shirts per month, Sai Sports India is well-equipped to handle large-scale orders. The company’s production facilities are designed for efficiency and high-volume output, allowing it to support the needs of both individual athletes and institutional clients.
